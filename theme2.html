Theme 2 - Project Taxi Driver

The idea about the movie

In theme 2, I worked in a group and our task was to create a Interactive Production based on one chosen movie. Our choice was a "Taxi Driver" directed by Martin Scorsese. This movie is a huge classic so working with it was a pleasure. It was our first group project and it was really challenging. Our ideas were diffrent but we all agreed about the intro to our production. The idea was to create a really short intro movie, so we meet on Wendesday to make it. We rented a car from the "Drive now" and bought smoke bombs. It was a simple plan but implementation was not simple. After three hours we finnaly made it, and we were so proud about it. We also did some pictures so later we created the animations from them. Working with this group was a good experience. You can see our final production from the link below.
